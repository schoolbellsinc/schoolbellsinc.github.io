How You Can Help Give: Our greatest need is an operating budget! We want to purchase a van to transport medicines and medical equipment around the country. In addition, we need to cover $2500 shipping costs for donated materials.

What your money can do …

$2000 brings electricity to a rural school
$350 allows a family to send their child to high school
$40 supports a student in elementary school for a year
$20 puts a blood sugar test in a diabetic hand
$300 places a laptop in a school

If you select one of the above options,100% of your donation will go to that choice. General donations will go towards paying for the annual shipment of goods.
