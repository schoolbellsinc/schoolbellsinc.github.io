Education The Belizean Educational System is based on British and Caribbean Models which divide schools into Primary, Secondary and Tertiary tiers. Children begin their eight years of primary education with two years of «Infant» classes, followed by six «standards». Secondary education is divided into four «forms». The sixth form of 2 years is the equivalent to Junior Colleges (Sixth Form) that provide post-secondary diplomas recognized in most American and Caribbean institutions of higher learning.

about

School Bells, Inc, a 501© 3 non-profit humanitarian organization was founded in 2003 with the mission to serve and provide essential life skills, educational support, economic growth, and health services for underserved communities. School Bells, Inc. accomplishes these goals through a variety of programs geared toward uplifting communities. School Bells, Inc. is dedicated to providing educational and medical services to Belize and her many villages. There are children with worms and skin disease, who are malnourished.
